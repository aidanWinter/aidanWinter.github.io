Who I Am

Hi, I'm Aidan. I'm a fourth year mechanical engineering student at Northeastern University. I've always wanted to be an engineer after spending countless hours as a kid making functioning roller coaster models. I have experience with welding, mills, lathes, soldering, and other tools in a machine shop. I also know how to use SolidWorks, AutoCAD, MATLAB, docSTAR, SAP, Minitab, LabVIEW, Propel, and Microsoft Office. I'm also an active member of NU Baja SAE and am a Chassis Team Design Lead. Some of my hobbies are skiing, bodybuilding, playing guitar, and I also train for Spartan race with NU Elite Heat.
