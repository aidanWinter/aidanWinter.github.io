PORTFOLIO

  • About Me

Barbell Board Press Clamp

A device that can mate around a barbell in the gym. Allows the user to shortern the range of motion while bench pressing, similar to a board press without a second person. Tested personally up to 335lbs of load (so far).

M Series Commercial Square Housing

The square trim housing in the M Series Commerical Lighting Family. Designed to work both with adjustable and downlight LED modules. The two surface finish keeps trims secure, while still providing a nice backing for the light.

M Series Adjustable Commercial Downlight

One of several downlights in the M Series Commerical Lighting Family. Designed to be capable of different lumen outputs and work with different drivers and various trims.

Two Piece Trim

A two piece trim capable of diffusing light from a round source through a square trim. Allows for use with multiple modules (such as an adjustable or downlight), as well as different variants such as an EM (emergency) variation with room for a user accesible button to restore power.

Revised Trim Spring

A new version of springs which provide the right amount of "bite" into the module housings to allow for the appropiate amount of force to remove the trims.

Wall-mounted Cylinder

A wall-mounted cylinder lamp which resists water up to an IP65 rating. Reworked to improve the seal, preventing water from leaking in.

3D Printed Mid Century Modern Smart Lamp

A 3D printed lamp designed to fit a mid century modern aesthetic. Powered by a DMF HMC2C LED module, hooked up to a smart switch.

Gym Pun Tee Shirts

A continously growing collection of gym shirt designs parodying pop culture with puns. Available for purchase on Amazon and soon TikTok Shop.

Xenon Headlight Upgrade

Halogen headlight assemblies replaced with xenon ones. Wiring pigtail stripped from junkyard, swapped in, additional cables added and attached to wiring harness with WAGOs and twist connects. Headlights coded in with BMW INPA tools to properly work.

Assembly Box

Box designed to be used in the manufacture of a component. Can make several components at once and was scaled down to create smaller ones.

Wall Sub-Assembly Drawing

A drawing from of wall sub-assembly from the box. Drawing follows standard GD&T conventions.

Scaled Down Assembly Box

A scaled down version of the assembly box used for creating a smaller version of the component

Bulkhead Section

One of several bulkhead sections that required extensive mathematics to ensure each cable had enough clearance between each other for safety requirements, all while fitting in a limited area.

Weighted Model (Large)

Model of component in 3D printer. Weighted properly to ensure center of gravity. FEA simulations performed to ensure it would react desired ways.

Weighted Model (Small)

Same weighted model with smaller weights to simulate same component with a mass change, but same center of gravity.

Embrittlement Test Rig

Cable Routing
